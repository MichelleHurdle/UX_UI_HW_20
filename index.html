Discovery

This team project involved redesigning and rebranding a non-profit wolf rescue website and app in order to increase visitor compliance with the rules and guidelines for interactions with the wolves (the primary means of non-profit funding), as well as to increase wolf sponsorships.

We collaborated with the non-profit owner/stakeholder to find out more about the rescue, it’s mission, stakeholder frustrations and funding needs. We wanted to understand what the website was primarily being used for and what the non-profit needed in order to be more successful.
